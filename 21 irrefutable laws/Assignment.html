This is my assignment based on the 21 Irrefutable laws of Leadership by John C. Maxwell

Summary:

John C. Maxwell does an amazing job in The 21 Irrefutable laws of Leadership, mentoring the reader in a way anyone can understand.
Debunking leadership showing its humility, beginning with The Law of the Lid which determines a persons level of effectiveness and accomplishments.
The second law, The Law of Influence shows readers that contrary to myths, leadership is not the same as management, entreprenueurship, knowledge, pioneership, or position in fact the proof is in the pudding a leader is defined by his followers. As a leaders followers wears his teachings representing him as does a athlete to a country.
The third law, TLO Process preaches that; Leadership develops daily, not in a day!
